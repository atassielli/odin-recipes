Beer Battered Fish

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts vegetable oil
  • (8) 4 oz cod fish fillets
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspooms ground black pepper
  • 1 egg beaten

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 365 degrees F.
  2. Rinse fish, pat dry, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Combine flour, garlic powder, paprika, 2 teaspoons salt, and 2 teaspoons pepper. Stir egg into dry ingredients.
  4. Gradually mix in one beer until a thin batter is formed.
  5. Dip fish fillets into the batter, then drop one at a time into hot oil.
  6. Fry fish, turning once, until both sides are golden brown.
  7. Drain on paper towels and serve warm.
